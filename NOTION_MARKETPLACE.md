# 📝 Notion Marketplace Submission Guide

**How to submit Product Hunt Launch Template to Notion Template Gallery**

---

## 🎯 Overview

Notion Template Gallery is a curated collection of community templates. Submission is free and can drive significant traffic to your template.

**Benefits:**
- 10-50 downloads/week (average template)
- Community exposure
- Potential for featured placement
- Build reputation in Notion community

---

## 📋 Prerequisites

Before submitting, ensure you have:

- [ ] **Notion account** (free or pro)
- [ ] **Template published** in your Notion workspace
- [ ] **Gallery images** (3-5 screenshots)
- [ ] **Template description** (150-200 characters)
- [ ] **Category** selected (Productivity, Business, Personal)
- [ ] **Tags** (3-5 relevant tags)

---

## 🚀 Submission Process

### Step 1: Prepare Template

1. **Create Notion page** with template structure
2. **Add placeholder content** to show how it works
3. **Include instructions** on how to use the template
4. **Test template** by duplicating it yourself
5. **Share template** with team for feedback

### Step 2: Create Gallery Images

**Requirements:**
- 3-5 screenshots
- 1920x1080px recommended
- PNG format
- Show key template features

**Tools:**
- macOS: Cmd+Shift+4 (screenshot)
- Windows: Win+Shift+S (snipping tool)
- Browser: Notion web interface

**What to capture:**
1. Overview section (show structure)
2. Pre-launch checklist (show timeline)
3. Launch day checklist (show schedule)
4. Metrics tracking (show tables)
5. Post-launch analysis (show review framework)

### Step 3: Write Description

**Template (150-200 characters):**
```
Complete Product Hunt launch system with 30-day pre-launch checklist, 
launch day schedule, real-time metrics tracking, and post-launch analysis. 
Used by successful founders for systematic launches.
```

**Alternative versions:**
```
Plan, execute, and analyze Product Hunt launches with this comprehensive 
template. Includes checklists, metrics tracking, engagement log, and 
iteration planning.
```

```
From pre-launch to post-launch analysis - everything you need for a 
successful Product Hunt launch. Checklists, schedules, metrics, and 
learnings in one place.
```

### Step 4: Submit to Notion

**Option 1: Notion Template Gallery Form (Recommended)**

1. Go to: https://www.notion.so/templates/submit
2. Sign in to your Notion account
3. Fill out submission form:
   - **Template name:** Product Hunt Launch Template
   - **Description:** (use one from above)
   - **Category:** Business / Productivity
   - **Tags:** product-hunt, launch, marketing, startup, template
   - **Gallery images:** Upload 3-5 screenshots
   - **Template link:** Share your Notion page
4. Submit for review

**Option 2: Notion Social Media**

1. Post template on Twitter/X with:
   - Screenshots of template
   - Description of features
   - Link to template
   - Tags: #notion #template #producthunt
2. Tag @NotionHQ for potential feature
3. Share in Notion communities

### Step 5: Post-Submission

**After submission:**
- [ ] Wait for review (1-7 days)
- [ ] Check email for approval
- [ ] Share approved template on social media
- [ ] Monitor downloads in Notion analytics
- [ ] Respond to user feedback
