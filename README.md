## 📋 Overview

This comprehensive Notion template helps you plan, execute, and analyze your Product Hunt launches. Used by autonomous AI company Auto Company for systematic launches.

**Features:**
- ✅ Complete pre-launch checklist (30-day timeline)
- ✅ Launch day minute-by-minute schedule
- ✅ Real-time metrics tracking
- ✅ Engagement log with response templates
- ✅ Outreach tracker for social media, email, communities
- ✅ Post-launch analysis framework
- ✅ Iteration planning for next launches

## 🎯 Quick Start

### Option 1: Manual Import (5 minutes)

1. Copy the entire `product-hunt-launch-template.md` file
2. Create a new page in Notion
3. Paste the content
4. Notion will automatically convert markdown to blocks
5. Start filling in your launch details

## 🚀 Usage Examples

### Example 1: First-Time Founder

**Scenario:** First-time founder launching MVP

**How to use:**
1. Start 30 days before launch
2. Complete "Pre-Launch Checklist" section
3. Follow daily tasks in timeline
4. Use "Launch Day Checklist" on launch day
5. Track real-time metrics in "Metrics Tracking"

**Result:** Systematic launch process, no missed opportunities

### Example 2: Serial Entrepreneur

**Scenario:** Experienced founder launching 5th product

**How to use:**
1. Skip to "Competitor Analysis" section
2. Focus on differentiation strategy
3. Use "Engagement Log" for comment templates
4. Track metrics in "Metrics Tracking"
5. Review "Post-Launch Analysis" for learnings

**Result:** Data-driven launch optimization

### Example 3: Team Launch

**Scenario:** Team of 3 launching product

**How to use:**
1. Assign roles in "Support Team" section
2. Divide checklist tasks among team members
3. Use "Outreach Tracker" for coordinated outreach
4. Monitor metrics together in "Metrics Tracking"
5. Debrief using "Post-Launch Analysis"

**Result:** Coordinated team effort, comprehensive coverage
